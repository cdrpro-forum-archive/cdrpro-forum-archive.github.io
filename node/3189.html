error message while opening a file

Try resetting your workspace. Press and hold down the F8 key while starting up CorelDRAW. Answer "Yes" to accepting the default workspace reset.

What may be happening, you may have a corrupt workspace. Removing and re-installing the Corel Graphics Suite will not remove and re-install the existing workspace. And that may be why you see the same error message after a re-installation.
